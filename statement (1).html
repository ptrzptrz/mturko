HOW YOUR RATING WORKS

Your rating uses Glicko-2, an improved version of the Elo system. Each format (Blitz, Rapid, Classic) has its own independent rating, all starting at 1500.

After every game your rating updates based on the result and your opponent's strength. Winning against stronger players gives you more points; losing to weaker opponents costs you more. The system also tracks how confident it is in your rating and how consistent your results are, which determines how much it moves after each game.

As you play more, the system becomes more confident in estimating your level. Rating changes are larger at the beginning and gradually smaller over time.
