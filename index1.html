Rating
How your rating works

Your rating uses Glicko-2, an evolution of Elo. Each format (Rapid, Blitz, and Classic) has its own rating, starting at 1500.

After each game, your rating changes based on the result and your opponent's strength. Beating stronger players gives more points, while losing to lower-rated players costs more.

Over time, as the system becomes more confident in your level, rating changes get smaller.
